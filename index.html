About Me

I am a researcher interested in bird ecology, biodiversity change, and emerging pollutants such as microplastics. My work integrates trait-based ecology, phylogenetic approaches, and global synthesis to understand vulnerability patterns across species.

Currently I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute of Urban Environment, Chinese Academy of Sciences.
